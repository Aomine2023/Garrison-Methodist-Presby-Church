Our Story

Garrison Methodist Presbyterian Church has been a cornerstone of faith in our community for over a century. Founded in 1892, our church has grown from a small gathering of believers to a vibrant community that welcomes all who seek to know God's love.

We are a unique congregation that embraces both Methodist and Presbyterian traditions, creating a rich tapestry of worship and fellowship that honors our diverse heritage while staying true to the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
